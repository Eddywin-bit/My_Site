Client: Burger King GH Role: Creative Lead Year: 2024

The goal was simple: Make the new spicy burger look so good it's impossible to ignore. We used warm lighting, "sweat" droplets on the soda cans, and dynamic angles to create a sense of movement and heat. The campaign ran for 3 weeks and resulted in the highest weekend sales record for the branch.
